Dice Game playing instructions:

1. Tell me your names. You can enter them below this instruction.

2. Click the "Roll" button to roll dices.

3. You can roll dices as many times as you want but be aware that you can lose your current round points any second!

4. You gain extra points when:

  • you roll two consecutive numbers (double points from this roll)

5. You lose all points from current roll when:

  • you roll a double
  • you roll a curse number (3)
  • you roll same two numbers you rolled in the previous roll

6. You win when you reach between 100 and 110 points

7. You lose 40 points when you reach higher than 110
